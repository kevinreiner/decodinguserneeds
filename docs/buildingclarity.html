Problem Space

Navigating the Chaos: Identifying Key Challenges

The existing Knowledge Center lacked clarity and structure, making it difficult for users to find relevant information. The main challenges included: (1) Poor navigation and an unclear categorization system (2) Inconsistent content quality, (3) an overloaded FAQ section that did not effectively guide users, (4) insufficient resources to help advertisers proactively prevent fraud. Given these issues, a strategic redesign was necessary to create a more intuitive, accessible, and user-centric knowledge repository.

Process

The redesign followed the Double Diamond framework, ensuring a structured and user-focused approach

01 Discover

The initial phase involved an extensive content audit to identify all existing topics within the Knowledge Center. To benchmark industry standards, we analyzed competitors' knowledge centers and identified three potential structuring frameworks: (1) Topic-based (organized by key subjects), (2) Role-based (tailored to different user groups), (3) Task-based (focused on specific actions users perform). Additionally, we conducted user research to understand target audiences, their needs, and motivations for using the Knowledge Center. These insights provided a foundation for the subsequent restructuring efforts.
